Massage Therapy

Help to relieve stress, pain and restrictions

Muscle Relaxations

Our massage therapy is felt as a gentle vibration within the soft tissues, which relaxes the underlying muscles, making soft tissues more pliable for manipulations aimed at increasing range of motion.

Increased Mobilization

The massaging effect created by Les into the patient’s tissues, causing pathways to open and increase the flow of blood, nutrients, and lymph throughout the body, helping to naturally heal damaged tissues.

Pain Relief

Once the flow of blood, oxygen, and nutrients is restored to muscles afflicted by adhesions from injury or microtrauma, the resulting pain is diminished, and mobility is increased.

ABOUT LES

Hi, my name is Lespaul Newton, owner of Lespaul Massage Therapy. I attended Swedish Institute in Manhattan and earned my license in massage therapy in 1997. My goal is to help people live better lives by utilizing the healing benefits of massage.

My passion is to help heal people. Regular bodywork is an important the key to living up to your fullest potential. Massage can be beneficial to everyone when applied in the correct manner by a caring, knowledgeable therapist. I’ve spent many years working in different types of massage environment from chiropractic, to high-end spa, and even corporate massage work. I bring all of that to one table, combining several modalities into one very unique style designed to get the best therapeutic results to fit the client’s needs.
